Advanced Seismic Retrofitting Techniques for Bridges

Author: Dr. Emily Chen

Published: June 2022

This paper presents a comprehensive study on advanced seismic retrofitting techniques for aging bridges. The research focuses on innovative methods such as base isolation, energy dissipation devices, and the use of fiber-reinforced polymers (FRP) to enhance the seismic resilience of bridge structures.

The study includes case studies of bridges retrofitted using these techniques, demonstrating significant improvements in their ability to withstand seismic events. The paper also discusses the cost-effectiveness and practicality of these methods for widespread implementation.
